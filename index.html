The
Intersection

Biological systems are the ultimate software. My work treats code not just as a tool, but as a lens to observe and simulate the complexity of the natural world.

As a Biology student at UBA and researcher at the Benthic Ecology Team (Exactas-UBA / CONICET), I specialize in the visual and computational study of cold seeps and chemosynthetic ecosystems.

By integrating high-fidelity photogrammetry from **ROV SuBastian** with autonomous orchestration protocols, I aim to bridge deep scientific curiosity with the technical tools that bring these stories to life.
